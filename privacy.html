Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

At Mkude, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Hospitality Marketing OS.

1. Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, subscribe to a plan, or use our AI generation tools. This may include your name, email address, business name, and payment information.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information to provide, maintain, and improve our services, process transactions, send technical notices, and respond to your comments and questions.

3. AI Content Generation

Inputs provided for AI content generation are processed by our AI partners (Anthropic and Google Gemini). We do not use your proprietary business data to train general models without your explicit consent.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption (AES-GCM) for sensitive data, to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Please contact us at support@mkude.com for such requests.
